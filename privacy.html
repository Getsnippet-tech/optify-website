Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address, display name, and authentication data when you create an account
  • Images: Photos you upload for editing, processed by our AI services
  • Usage data: App interactions, feature usage, and crash reports to improve the service
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Subscription data: Purchase history managed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the App's editing and AI services
  • Process your images through our AI pipeline
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Send important service notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the App
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Image Processing & Storage

When you upload an image, it is:

  • Transmitted securely over HTTPS to our servers
  • Processed by our AI pipeline to perform the requested edit
  • Stored temporarily in secure cloud storage (Google Cloud) with time-limited access URLs
  • Not used to train AI models
  • Not shared with third parties for their own purposes

Processed images are retained for a limited time. You can request deletion at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Authentication and database
  • Google Cloud Storage: Secure image storage
  • OpenAI, BFL, Replicate: AI image processing
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Sentry: Error monitoring and crash reporting

5. Data Security

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication with JWT tokens
  • Row-level security on database records
  • Time-limited signed URLs for image access
  • Regular security reviews

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to withdraw consent. Contact optify@getsnippet.co to exercise these rights.

7. Children's Privacy

Optify is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. Images are retained temporarily (up to 30 days) then deleted. Account deletion removes all data within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will notify you of significant changes via the App or email.

10. Contact

Questions? Email optify@getsnippet.co
