AI Personas let you transform your photos into 130+ cinematic styles — from Red Carpet and Yacht Party to Horoscope signs and fashion looks. Upload a selfie and watch the magic happen.

Optify supports JPEG, PNG, and HEIC images. For best results, use high-resolution photos with good lighting.
Most edits complete in 10–30 seconds depending on the operation. Complex tasks like AI Personas or Virtual Try-On may take up to 60 seconds.
